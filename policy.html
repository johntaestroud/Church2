Privacy Policy

Effective as of: January 16, 2026

The Church of God of St. Petersburg is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care and integrity.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you visit or interact with our website:

a. Personal Information
  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Mailing address (if provided)
  • Prayer requests or messages you submit
b. Technical Information
  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Website usage data (via cookies or analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Respond to inquiries or prayer requests
  • Send church updates, newsletters, or announcements
  • Deliver SMS notifications you have opted into
  • Improve website functionality and ministry outreach

We use your information only for church-related purposes.

3. SMS Messaging & Consent

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive text messages from the Church of God of St. Petersburg.

  • Message frequency may vary
  • Message and data rates may apply
  • You may opt out at any time by replying STOP

We do not sell or share phone numbers for marketing purposes.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With trusted subcontractors or service providers that support church operations (such as website hosting, SMS delivery platforms, or customer support services)
  • When required by law

All subcontractors and service providers are required to protect your information and use it only for the services they provide to the Church.

All other use case categories explicitly exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent. This information will not be shared with any third parties under any circumstances.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies or analytics tools to understand website usage and improve user experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no method of online transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

This website is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent.

8. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request corrections or deletion of your information
  • Opt out of communications at any time

Please contact us to make a request.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
